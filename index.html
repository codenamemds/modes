About Mars D. Silver

My name is Mars Delavonta Silver - I am an artist, designer, cultural curator, and tech professional born and bred on the South Side of Chicago, IL.

I specialize in creating digital experiences that bridge the gap between art, technology, and culture. Through MODES-X, I explore the intersection of design thinking and technological innovation.

My work spans across multiple disciplines including web development, graphic design, music composition, and cultural curation, always with a focus on authentic storytelling and community engagement.
